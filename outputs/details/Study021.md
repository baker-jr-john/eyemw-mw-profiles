# Study 021 – Eye Mind Wander Database: Complete Dataset Details

## Source
Pagkratidou, M., Scattarelli, J., Mills, C. & Kendeou, P. presented research titled "Exploring the effects of off-task thought during reading of refutation texts" as an oral presentation at the 34th Society for Text & Discourse Annual Conference in Chicago, Illinois, 2024.

## Technical Specifications

**Eye Tracking Equipment:** Webcam-based tracking using WebGazer technology

**Participant Recruitment:** Prolific platform

**Primary Task:** Participants engaged in reading a text presented one paragraph at a time

**Task Classification:** Reading-based study

**Performance Measurement:** Not applicable (N/A)

## Data Collection Measures

**Mind Wandering Assessment:**
- Task-Unrelated Thought (TUT) measurement
- Freely-reported Mind-Wander Thought (FMT)
- Valence rating

**Gaze-Related Metrics:**
- Gazes recorded
- Unique gazes tracked
- Offscreen gazes documented
- Area of Interest (AOI) gazes measured

**Temporal Windows:**
- Gaze measurement window spans 3 paragraphs preceding probe administration
- AOI window focuses on the text paragraph being read
