# Study 013 – Eye Mind Wander Database: Complete Dataset Details

## Source
Steadman, C., Wong, A., Wei, Z., Baker, R.S., Mills, C. (2025). "Too hard or just right? How difficulty affects student engagement" [Poster Presentation]. Winter Text & Discourse Conference, Boulder, Colorado, United States.

## Detailed Specifications

**Eye Tracking Equipment:** Webcam-based system (WebGazer)

**Participant Recruitment:** Prolific platform

**Primary Task:** Participants viewed an educational video

**Task Classification:** Video-based study

**Performance Assessment:** Not applicable

**Mind Wandering Assessment Methods:** Task-Unrelated Thought (TUT), Disengagement ratings, Valence measurement, Boredom assessment

**Gaze Analysis Metrics:** Total gazes, Unique gaze instances, Off-screen gaze frequency, Area-of-Interest (AOI) gaze counts

**Temporal Analysis Window:** Data analyzed across 20-second intervals preceding probe presentation

**Area of Interest:** The video itself
