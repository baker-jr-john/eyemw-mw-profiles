# Study 004 – Eye Mind Wander Database: Complete Dataset Details

## Source Information
**Citation:** Jaiyeola, G. D., Wong, A. Y., Bryck, R. L., Mills, C., & Hutt, S. (2025, March). "One size does not fit all: Considerations when using webcam-based eye tracking to models of neurodivergent learners' attention and comprehension." In Proceedings of the 15th International Learning Analytics and Knowledge Conference (pp. 24-35). https://doi.org/10.1145/3706468.3706472

## Dataset Specifications

**Eye Tracker Technology:** Webcam-based (WebGazer)

**Participant Recruitment:** Prolific platform

**Primary Task:** Participants read text presented sequentially, one paragraph at a time

**Task Classification:** Reading comprehension activity

**Performance Measurement:** Not applicable for this dataset

**Mind Wandering Assessment Methods:**
- Task-Unrelated Thought (TUT)
- Awareness measures
- Focused Moment Test (FMT)
- Disengagement indicators

**Gaze Data Collected:**
- Total gazes
- Unique gazes
- Offscreen gazes
- Area of Interest (AOI) gazes

**Gaze Analysis Window:** Data from 2 paragraphs preceding each probe question

**Area of Interest (AOI) Definition:** Text paragraph being read
