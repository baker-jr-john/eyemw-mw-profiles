# Study 023 – Eye Mind Wander Database: Complete Dataset Details

## Source
Athilat, V., Wong, A., Sivakumar, H., Öncel, P., Braasch, J., Mills, C., & Allen, L.K. "Contextual Factors in Multiple Document Comprehension: A Replication and Extension of Kessler et al., 2021" [Poster session]. Winter Text and Discourse Conference, Boulder, CO, United States, February 3-5, 2025.

## Technical Specifications

**Eye Tracking Equipment:** Webcam (WebGazer)

**Participant Recruitment:** Prolific

**Task Description:** Read a text one paragraph at a time

**Task Classification:** Reading

**Performance Measurement:** N/A

## Measurement Details

**Mind Wandering Measures:**
- TUT (Task Unrelated Thoughts)
- Valence

**Gaze Measures:**
- Gazes
- Unique Gazes
- Offscreen Gazes
- AOI Gazes

**Gaze Measurement Window:** 3 paragraphs before probe

**Area of Interest (AOI) Window:** Text paragraph
