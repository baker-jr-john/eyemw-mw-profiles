# Study 006 – Eye Mind Wander Database: Complete Dataset Details

## Details

**Eye Tracker Technology:** Webcam-based system using WebGazer

**Participant Population:** Undergraduate students

**Primary Task:** Participants read text presented one paragraph at a time

**Task Classification:** Reading comprehension activity

**Performance Metric:** Total problems answered

**Mind Wandering Assessment Methods:**
- TUT (Task Unrelated Thought)
- Arousal measurements
- Valence measurements

**Gaze Measures:** Gazes

**Gaze Measure Window:** The paragraph immediately preceding probe presentations

**Areas of Interest (AOI):** Not applicable
