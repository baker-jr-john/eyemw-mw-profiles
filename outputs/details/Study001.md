# Study 001 – Eye Mind Wander Database: Complete Dataset Details

## Source
Hutt, S., Wong, A., Papoutsaki, A., Baker, R. S., Gold, J. I., & Mills, C. (2024). "Webcam-based eye tracking to detect mind wandering and comprehension errors." *Behavior Research Methods*, 56(1), 1-17. https://doi.org/10.3758/s13428-022-02040-x

## Details Section

**Eye Tracker:** Webcam (WebGazer)

**Participant Source:** Prolific

**Task Description:** An inference completion task wherein participants read or listened to a narrative and selected the option that best completes the story.

**Task Type:** Reading, Listening

**Performance Measure:** Inference completion accuracy

**Mind Wandering Measures:** TUT (Thought content sampling methodology)

**Gaze Measures:**
- Gazes
- Unique gazes
- Offscreen gazes
- AOI gazes

**Gaze Measure Window:** Prior sentence

**Area of Interest (AOI):** Three answer options
