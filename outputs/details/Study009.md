# Study 009 – Eye Mind Wander Database: Complete Dataset Details

## Study Information
**Title:** A novel Quantitative Assessment of engagement in virtual reality: Task-unrelated thought is reduced compared to 2D videos.

**Authors:** Kuvar V.K., Bailenson J.N. & Mills C.

**Publication:** Computers & Education (2023)

**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1016/j.compedu.2023.104959

---

## Methodology Details

**Eye Tracking Equipment:**
- Commercial system: Tobii TX300
- VR system: HP Omnicept Reverb G2

**Participant Demographics:** Undergraduates

**Experimental Task:** Participants watched an educational video

**Task Classification:** Video-based study

**Performance Measurement:** Not applicable (N/A)

**Mind Wandering Assessment Measures:**
- TUT (Task-Unrelated Thought)
- FMT (Focused Mentation Task)
- Disengagement
- Valence

**Gaze Analysis Metrics:** Fixations

**Temporal Analysis Window:** 20 seconds preceding probe administration

**Areas of Interest (AOI):** Not applicable (N/A)
