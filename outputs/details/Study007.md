# Study 007 – Eye Mind Wander Database: Complete Dataset Details

## Details

**Eye Tracking Technology:** Webcam-based system utilizing WebGazer

**Participant Recruitment:** Prolific platform

**Primary Task:** Participants watched a mathematical instructional video

**Task Classification:** Video-based study

**Performance Assessment:** Measured by total number of problems participants answered correctly

**Mind Wandering Assessment Methods:**
- TUT (Task Unrelated Thought)
- Disengagement metrics
- Valence measurements
- Boredom indicators

**Gaze Analysis Metrics:**
- Total gazes recorded
- Unique gaze instances
- Offscreen gaze tracking
- AOI (Area of Interest) gaze data

**Temporal Window for Analysis:** 20 seconds preceding probe presentations

**Area of Interest (AOI) Focus:** The video content itself
