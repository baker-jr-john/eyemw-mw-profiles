# Study 020 – Eye Mind Wander Database: Complete Dataset Details

## Source
Öncel, P., Kuvar, V., Medina-Perez, G., Mills, C., & Allen, L. K. (2024, November 21-24). "Investigating phenomenological experiences of reading in first versus second language" [Poster presentation]. 65th Annual Meeting of The Psychonomic Society, New York City, NY, USA.

## Dataset Specifications

**Eye Tracking Equipment:** Webcam (WebGazer)

**Participant Recruitment:** Prolific

**Primary Task:** Reading text presented one paragraph sequentially

**Task Classification:** Reading comprehension study

**Performance Assessment:** Not applicable

**Mind Wandering Assessment Method:** Thought-Unrelated-to-Task (TUT) probes

**Gaze Metrics Collected:**
- Gazes
- Unique gazes
- Offscreen gazes
- Area-of-Interest (AOI) gazes

**Temporal Analysis Window for Gaze Measures:** Three paragraphs preceding each probe

**AOI Definition:** The text paragraph participants were reading
