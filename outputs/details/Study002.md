# Study 002 – Eye Mind Wander Database: Complete Dataset Details

## Source
Hutt, S., Wong, A., Papoutsaki, A., Baker, R. S., Gold, J. I., & Mills, C. (2024). "Webcam-based eye tracking to detect mind wandering and comprehension errors." *Behavior Research Methods*, 56(1), 1-17. https://doi.org/10.3758/s13428-022-02040-x

## Detailed Specifications

**Eye Tracking Technology:** Webcam-based system using WebGazer

**Participant Recruitment:** Prolific platform

**Primary Task:** Inference completion assignment where participants either read or listened to narrative content and selected the most appropriate option to complete the story

**Task Modalities:** Reading and Listening conditions

**Performance Metric:** Accuracy on inference completion task responses

**Mind Wandering Assessment:** Thought-Probing on-task cognition (TUT) questionnaire

**Gaze-Related Measurements:**
- Total gazes
- Unique gazes (distinct fixation points)
- Offscreen gazes (looks away from content)
- AOI-specific gazes (focused on designated regions)

**Temporal Analysis Window:** Gaze data captured during the immediately preceding sentence

**Areas of Interest (AOI):** The three answer options presented to participants
